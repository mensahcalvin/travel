Our Story

Founded in 2010, ExploreWorld began as a small team of passionate travelers with a simple mission: to make world exploration accessible to everyone.

What started as a local tour operator has grown into an internationally recognized travel company with operations in over 30 countries. We've helped more than 50,000 travelers discover new destinations and create unforgettable memories.

Our commitment to sustainable tourism and authentic experiences sets us apart in the industry.

Our Mission

"To inspire and enable people to explore the world's cultures, landscapes, and communities in a meaningful and responsible way."
